Mr. Sunil RK (CEO)

3E-SERVICES was established in the year 1996 to provide services in Engineering, Education and Execution in construction. Founder & CEO Mr. Sunil R.K, has good academic background of B.E (Civil), M.E. (Structural Engineering) and also completed many continuing education programs & few are listed below.

  • PGDM

  • Dip in 'Industrial Safety'.

  • Proficiency in 'Project Management & Communication' from IISc, Bangalore.

  • Certificate of Proficiency course on NDT Level-II as per American Society for Non Destructive Testing (ASNT).

  • Advance Course on 'Health Assessment of Structures', SERC, Chennai.

  • Earth Quake resistant design of Structures.

  • Certificate Course on 'Concrete Technology' & Building supervision.

  • Trained in a Indo-Norwegian Programme on " Seismic Design of Multi-Storey Buildings : IS 1893 vs. Eurocode 8

  • Undergone a Training Programme on Waterproofing in new RCC buildings - Specifications , Materials and Methodology conducted by Dr. Fixit Institute

  • He is also a visiting faculty in Institute for Research Development and Training of Construction Trades and Management (INSTRUCT), Bangalore.

Mission


  • To become most preferred and leading contractor to cater for Rehabilitation & Retrofitting of Civil Engineering structures in India.

  • To educate the society in construction to reduce and manage the defects for prolonged life of the structures

  • To educate, orient & connect Civil Engineering students to Construction industry through knowledge sharing.

  • To educate construction workforce for better buildings in India.

Vision


  • To support Society and Industry through value based services which will increase and support the life of the Civil Engineering Structures.
